This page will give you a printable copy of your Trello Board, lists - cards - labels - desc - comments - votes. This is helpful even when you just want to be able to scan quickly, without having to turn each card over.

You may safely use this for private/proprietary data as no information leaves this browser page.

Step.1 - Import your Trello Board contents
  1. in another window or tab, access your Trello Board
  2. click show sidebar (top-right)
  3. right sidebar -> Menu -> Share,Print,Export -> Export JSON
  4. after the new JSON data appears, COPY the JSON data to your browser clipboard
  5. use Ctrl-V to PASTE your Trello Board's JSON into this box:

Step.2 - review/set your printing preferences

Saving your printing preferences You may save these printing preferences with your Trello board:
  • Create a card with title:
  • Add the json below as a COMMENT, and change the nickname field to something meaningful to you.
